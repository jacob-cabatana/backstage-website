FAQ

Quick answers.

What is BCKSTGE?
A modern nightlife ticketing and events platform for hosts, promoters, bars, clubs, and venues. Built to run the whole night from a single dashboard.
What is Bar Dashboard?
A live dashboard for mobile drink orders, event sales, payouts, marketing, and venue performance — all in one place, in real time.
What is Mobile Drink Ordering?
Mobile drink ordering built into Backstage. Guests order and pay from their phone, track the order live, and pick it up at a chosen spot — and every purchase adds them to the venue's audience.
Does BCKSTGE take a cut of ticket sales?
No. Hosts keep the full face value of every ticket sold. Guest-paid fees cover the platform and payment processing.
When are sales paid out?
Payouts are processed after the event ends so settlement stays clean and accurate.
Can venues use BCKSTGE?
Yes. BCKSTGE supports event pages, digital ticketing, guest check-in, promoter tracking, and reporting for any venue size.
