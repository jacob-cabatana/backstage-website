Frequently Asked Questions

Everything guests, hosts, and venues need to know about BCKSTGE.

What is BCKSTGE?

BCKSTGE is a mobile-first ticketing and event platform built for nightlife, live music, and social events. We help hosts create events, sell tickets, manage guests, and run check-in through one streamlined platform.

Who is BCKSTGE for?

BCKSTGE is built for event hosts, promoters, venues, artists, DJs, nightlife brands, and anyone looking for a simple way to manage ticketed events.

How does ticketing work?

Hosts can create an event, set ticket types and pricing, and share the event page with guests. Guests purchase tickets online and receive digital tickets for entry.

What are your ticketing fees?

BCKSTGE uses a transparent fee structure. Our ticketing fee includes BCKSTGE's service fee and standard credit card processing of 2.9% + $0.30. Guests always see the final total before checkout.

Does BCKSTGE take a cut of host ticket sales?

No. Hosts keep the face value revenue from their ticket sales. BCKSTGE's guest-paid ticketing fee covers the platform service fee and payment processing.

How does check-in work at the event?

Tickets are digital and can be checked in at the door using BCKSTGE's guest list and ticket scanning tools. This helps make entry faster and easier for hosts, venues, and guests.

Can hosts view ticket sales and guest information?

Yes. Hosts can track ticket sales, guest counts, and event performance through BCKSTGE's host tools. Event data can also support planning, staffing, settlement, and reporting.

When are ticket sales paid out?

Ticket payouts are processed after the event ends. This helps protect guests, hosts, and venues while keeping event settlement clear and organized.

Can BCKSTGE support venues?

Yes. BCKSTGE can provide event pages, digital ticketing, guest check-in, ticket reporting, and event visibility. We are open to working with venues to make sure their operational needs are met.

Can BCKSTGE provide ticket reporting?

Yes. BCKSTGE can provide ticket counts, guest information, sales performance, and other event data to help hosts and venue teams stay informed before and after the event.

Does BCKSTGE help promote events?

BCKSTGE gives hosts a clean event page and shareable ticket link that can be used across social media, ads, text campaigns, and other marketing channels. Select events may also receive additional promotional support through BCKSTGE.

Can guests receive digital tickets?

Yes. Guests receive digital tickets after purchase and can use them for entry at the event.

Can hosts offer complimentary tickets?

Yes. Hosts can manage guest access through BCKSTGE, including paid tickets, guest list access, and complimentary entry options depending on the event setup.

Can BCKSTGE handle different ticket types?

Yes. Hosts can create different ticket options such as general admission, early-entry tickets, anytime access, VIP options, gender-based pricing, and volume-based ticket releases depending on the event.

How do guests contact support?

Guests can contact BCKSTGE for help with ticket-related questions, order issues, or event access support.
