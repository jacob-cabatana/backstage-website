Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Party Lux LLC, doing business as BCKSTGE, Backstage, and Bckstge, collects, uses, shares, stores, and protects information when you access or use our websites, mobile applications, ticketing tools, event pages, venue dashboards, host dashboards, Fast Lane ordering tools, Pour Decisions tools, messaging features, payment flows, and related services.

By using BCKSTGE, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not access or use BCKSTGE.

1. Who We Are

BCKSTGE is operated by Party Lux LLC. In this Privacy Policy, “BCKSTGE,” “Backstage,” “Bckstge,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to Party Lux LLC and the products, websites, mobile applications, dashboards, tools, and services we operate.

“Platform” means the BCKSTGE website, mobile application, event tools, ticketing system, venue dashboard, host dashboard, Fast Lane ordering system, Pour Decisions tools, pickup code system, crowd insight tools, messaging tools, and any related services.

2. Information We Collect

Account and Profile Information

We may collect information you provide when creating or using an account, including your name, display name, username, email address, phone number, profile photo, account status, login information, and optional profile details.

If you choose to provide social profile information, such as an Instagram handle, we may store and use that information to support profile or social features inside the Platform.

Guest Mode Information

You may be able to use parts of BCKSTGE without creating an account. Guest users may still generate limited device, usage, ticket, checkout, order, transaction, or event interaction data when using public features.

Some features, including ticket claiming, saved activity, event hosting, purchases, order history, or account related features, may require additional information.

Ticketing and Event Information

We may collect event registrations, ticket purchases, claimed tickets, free ticket claims, ticket type, ticket quantity, QR codes, acquire codes, order codes, check in status, event access records, event details, host information, promoter information, fraternity information, venue information, and related transaction records.

If you submit information through checkout or ticket claim flows, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, payment status, event selection, and other information needed to provide access to the event.

Fast Lane Drink Ordering Information

If you use Fast Lane or Pour Decisions ordering features, we may collect venue selected, menu items, item quantities, notes, pickup spot, pickup instructions, order status, order code, subtotal, service fees, tips, taxes if applicable, payment status, refund status, and related order lifecycle information.

Order lifecycle information may include pending, accepted, ready, picked up, cancelled, refunded, or failed status updates.

Payment and Financial Information

Payments may be processed through third party payment processors, including Stripe and Apple Pay. We do not store full credit card numbers, debit card numbers, or CVV codes on our servers.

We may receive payment metadata such as transaction ID, payment status, amount paid, card brand, last four digits, refund status, chargeback status, receipt information, payout records, and related transaction details.

Messaging and Communication Information

We may collect phone numbers, email addresses, SMS consent records, opt in status, opt out status, message delivery records, support messages, emails, form submissions, and other communications you send to us.

We may use this information to send ticket confirmations, acquire codes, order updates, event updates, receipts, account notices, support replies, safety messages, and security alerts.

Location Information

If you grant permission, we may collect approximate or precise location information to show nearby venues, nearby events, venue distance, pickup eligibility, timing guidance, wait time estimates, crowd related insights, or location based features.

You can disable location access through your device settings at any time. Some features may not work properly without location access.

Device, Usage, and Technical Information

We may collect device model, operating system, app version, browser type, IP address, language, region, crash logs, diagnostics, performance data, pages viewed, screens viewed, buttons tapped, features used, venue interactions, event interactions, and other usage information.

We may use cookies, local storage, session identifiers, and similar technologies to remember preferences, support login sessions, improve performance, analyze usage, and protect the Platform.

Camera, Photos, Media, and Audio Recognition

If you choose to use camera, photo, media, or upload features, we may process the content you capture or upload to provide those features. You control camera and photo permissions through your device settings.

If BCKSTGE offers music recognition or “Now Playing” features, we may use Apple frameworks such as ShazamKit to identify music and display track metadata. We do not use this feature to intentionally record private conversations.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to provide, operate, maintain, protect, and improve BCKSTGE.

  1. Create and manage accounts, guest sessions, tickets, orders, event access, venue dashboards, and host dashboards.
  2. Process ticket purchases, Fast Lane orders, payments, refunds, service fees, tips, receipts, and transaction records.
  3. Send ticket confirmations, acquire codes, claim codes, order updates, event updates, account notifications, support messages, receipts, and security alerts.
  4. Show nearby venues, nearby events, estimated crowd information, wait time guidance, timing suggestions, and location based recommendations.
  5. Prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, chargebacks, duplicate ticket claims, duplicate free ticket claims, security incidents, and misuse of the Platform.
  6. Debug errors, analyze performance, improve design, develop features, and maintain service reliability.
  7. Comply with legal, tax, accounting, payment processing, platform, and regulatory obligations.
  8. Enforce our Terms, protect users, protect venues, protect hosts, protect payment processors, and protect BCKSTGE.

4. SMS, Email, and Notification Consent

BCKSTGE may send SMS messages, emails, push notifications, or similar communications related to your use of the Platform.

Transactional Messages

Transactional messages may include ticket confirmations, acquire codes, claim codes, event access information, Fast Lane order status updates, receipts, payment confirmations, refund updates, support updates, account notices, safety notices, and security alerts.

Marketing or Promotional Messages

We may send marketing or promotional messages only where permitted by law and where appropriate consent has been collected. You may opt out of marketing communications without affecting your ability to receive important transactional messages where allowed by law.

SMS Opt In

By entering your phone number and agreeing to receive messages, you consent to receive recurring automated and non automated text messages from BCKSTGE. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

SMS Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP. You may request help by replying HELP. After opting out, you may still receive non SMS communications or legally required notices.

Push Notifications and Email

You can manage push notification permissions in your device settings. You may unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link where provided or by contacting us. We may still send transactional, legal, safety, or account related emails.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in limited circumstances described below.

Service Providers

We may share information with service providers that help us operate BCKSTGE, including payment processors, cloud providers, database providers, hosting providers, analytics providers, authentication providers, email providers, SMS providers, notification providers, support providers, fraud prevention providers, and security providers.

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, legal process, law enforcement request, regulatory requirement, payment processor request, or to protect the rights, safety, property, or security of BCKSTGE, users, hosts, venues, service providers, or the public.
