Privacy Policy for BCKSTGE

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

BCKSTGE, also referred to as “Backstage,” “Bckstge,” “we,” “our,” or “us,” respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our mobile application, websites, event pages, ticketing tools, messaging features, venue tools, Fast Lane ordering features, and related services collectively referred to as the “Service.”

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account and Profile Information

  • Name, display name, username, email address, phone number, profile photo, and other information you choose to provide.
  • Login, authentication, and account status information.
  • Optional social profile information, such as an Instagram handle, if you choose to provide it.

Guest Mode Information

  • You may use certain parts of the Service without creating an account.
  • Guest users may still generate limited device, usage, order, ticket, or transaction data when interacting with public events, checkout pages, or app features.
  • Some features, including ticket claiming, saved activity, event hosting, purchases, or order history, may require additional information.

Ticketing and Event Information

  • Event registrations, ticket purchases, claimed tickets, free ticket claims, QR codes, acquire codes, order codes, check in status, and related event access records.
  • Information submitted during checkout, including name, email address, phone number, ticket type, quantity, event details, and payment status.
  • Host, promoter, fraternity, venue, or business information submitted through BCKSTGE hosting tools.

Fast Lane Drink Ordering Information

  • Venue selected, menu items, quantities, notes, pickup spot, pickup instructions, order status, order code, subtotal, service fees, tips, taxes if applicable, and payment status.
  • Order lifecycle data, such as pending, accepted, ready, picked up, cancelled, refunded, or failed.
  • Information visible to the venue or bartender dashboard so the venue can prepare and fulfill your order.

Payments and Financial Information

  • We use third party payment processors, including Stripe and Apple Pay, to process payments.
  • We do not store full credit card numbers, debit card numbers, or CVV codes on our servers.
  • We may receive payment metadata such as transaction ID, payment status, amount paid, card brand, last four digits, refund status, chargeback status, and receipt information.

Messaging and Communication Information

  • Phone numbers and email addresses used to send ticket confirmations, acquire codes, order updates, account notices, event updates, receipts, support replies, and security messages.
  • SMS consent records, including opt in status, opt out status, timestamp, source of consent, and message delivery records.
  • Support messages, emails, forms, or other communications you send to us.

Location Information

  • If you grant permission, we may collect approximate or precise location information to show nearby venues, events, wait time estimates, venue distance, pickup eligibility, or location based features.
  • You can disable location permissions through your device settings at any time.
  • Some features may not work properly without location access.

Device, Usage, and Technical Information

  • Device model, operating system, app version, browser type, IP address, language, region, crash logs, diagnostics, and performance data.
  • Pages, screens, features, buttons, events, venues, or actions viewed or used inside the Service.
  • Cookies, local storage, session identifiers, and similar technologies used to remember preferences, support login sessions, improve performance, and protect the Service.

Camera, Photos, and Media

  • If you choose to use camera, photo, or media features, we may process the content you capture or upload to provide that feature.
  • You control whether the app has access to your camera or photo library through your device settings.

Audio Recognition

  • If BCKSTGE offers music recognition or “Now Playing” features, we may use Apple frameworks such as ShazamKit.
  • Where supported, audio recognition is intended to identify music and display track metadata.
  • We do not use this feature to intentionally record private conversations.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Create and manage accounts, guest sessions, tickets, orders, event access, venue dashboards, and host tools.
  • Process ticket purchases, Fast Lane orders, payments, refunds, fees, receipts, and transaction records.
  • Send ticket confirmations, acquire codes, order updates, event updates, account notifications, support messages, and security alerts.
  • Show nearby venues, events, estimated crowd information, wait time guidance, timing suggestions, and location based recommendations.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, chargebacks, duplicate ticket claims, and security incidents.
  • Debug errors, analyze performance, improve product design, and develop new features.
  • Comply with legal, tax, accounting, payment processing, and regulatory obligations.
  • Enforce our terms, protect users, protect venues, protect hosts, and protect BCKSTGE.

3. SMS, Email, and Notification Consent

BCKSTGE may send you SMS messages, emails, push notifications, or similar communications related to your use of the Service.

Transactional Messages

  • Ticket confirmations.
  • Acquire codes or claim codes.
  • Event access information.
  • Fast Lane order status updates.
  • Receipts, payment confirmations, refunds, or support updates.
  • Important account, safety, security, or service messages.

Marketing or Promotional Messages

  • We may send marketing or promotional messages only where permitted by law and where appropriate consent has been collected.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications without affecting your ability to receive important transactional messages where allowed by law.

SMS Opt In

By entering your phone number and agreeing to receive messages, you consent to receive recurring automated and non automated text messages from BCKSTGE. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

SMS Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP. You may request help by replying HELP. After opting out, you may still receive non SMS communications or legally required notices.

Push Notifications

You can manage push notification permissions in your device settings.

Email

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link where provided or by contacting us. We may still send transactional, legal, or account related emails.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Payment processors, including Stripe and Apple Pay.
  • Cloud, database, hosting, analytics, authentication, and infrastructure providers, including Firebase and Google Cloud services.
  • Email, SMS, notification, customer support, fraud prevention, and security providers.
  • App platform providers, including Apple, when necessary for app distribution, payments, notifications, or device functionality.

Venues, Hosts, and Event Organizers

  • When you buy, claim, or use a ticket, we may share relevant ticket and attendee information with the event host or organizer.
  • When you place a Fast Lane order, we may share your order details, pickup information, and order code with the venue so the order can be fulfilled.
  • Hosts and venues are expected to use this information only for event access, order fulfillment, safety, support, and related operational purposes.

Legal, Safety, and Compliance

  • We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, legal process, law enforcement request, or regulatory requirement.
  • We may disclose information to protect the rights, safety, property, or security of BCKSTGE, users, hosts, venues, payment processors, or the public.

Business Transfers

  • If BCKSTGE is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, sale of assets, or similar transaction, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Payments

  • Payments are processed by third party payment processors, including Stripe and Apple Pay.
  • BCKSTGE does not store full card numbers or CVV codes on our servers.
  • Payment processors may collect and process payment information according to their own privacy policies and terms.
  • We may store transaction records, receipt details, refund records, payout records, fee records, and chargeback information for accounting, fraud prevention, support, and legal compliance.

6. Data Retention

  • Account information is generally retained while your account remains active.
  • Guest, ticket, order, transaction, event, and venue records may be retained as needed for access control, support, fraud prevention, dispute handling, tax, accounting, and legal compliance.
  • Payment and transaction records may be retained for legally required accounting and tax periods.
  • Diagnostic, analytics, and usage records may be retained for a reasonable period to improve reliability, security, and product performance.
  • If you request deletion, we will delete or anonymize eligible information unless we need to retain it for legal, safety, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute resolution, or legitimate business purposes.

  • SMS opt out: Reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages.
  • Email opt out: Use the unsubscribe link where available or contact us.
