Why it wins

A wedge into the whole night.

Fast Lane isn't a feature — it's the transaction layer for nightlife.

Take rate

Revenue on every drink

Backstage already takes guests from discovery to ticket. Fast Lane extends the same rails to the highest-frequency transaction in a venue — the drink order — with a clean 10% + $1 take rate the guest pays.

Owned audience

A CRM that fills itself

Every purchase enrolls a guest in that venue's audience, turning one-time foot traffic into a re-marketable list. Bars get a direct push channel for specials and events — and the more they message, the more reasons guests have to stay on the platform.

Two-sided moat

Venues and guests reinforce each other

Every venue that turns on Fast Lane brings its crowd; every guest who skips a line expects it at the next bar. Audience tools, live presence, and pickup routing get stickier the more a venue uses them.

Platform

One stack, expanding surface area

Tickets, drink ordering, audience messaging, promoter payouts, and venue analytics already run on a single Firebase + Stripe backend. Each new module raises revenue per venue without raising the cost to serve them.
